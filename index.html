How it works

Three steps from sign-up to live.

No custom development. No long integration cycles. Configure your competitions, paste one line of code, and your users are predicting.

STEP 01
Configure your competitions

Use the admin panel to set up competitions for any league or tournament. Create rounds, assign matches, choose your game mode, and set deadlines. No technical knowledge required.

STEP 02
Embed with one line

Copy your personalised iframe snippet from the dashboard and paste it anywhere on your site — a widget, a dedicated page, a sidebar. It runs entirely on your users' screens, styled to match your brand.

Game modes

Three ways to predict.
One platform.

Every competition runs one of four formats. Pick the one that fits your audience, or run all four simultaneously.

🎯
Last Man Standing

Users pick one result per event. One wrong prediction and they're out. Games resolve in kick-off order, building tension through the entire gameweek. The last survivor wins.

HIGH TENSION SEASONAL ELIMINATION
📊
Score Predictor

Users predict the exact scoreline for every match in the round. Exact scores earn maximum points, correct results earn partial credit. Rewards knowledge and rewards accuracy.

HIGH SKILL WEEKLY OR SEASON TIERED POINTS
🎰
Betting Markets

Users predict multiple markets per match — 1X2 result, Over/Under 2.5 goals, and Both Teams to Score. You choose which markets to activate and set their own points for each. Maximum engagement per fixture.

MULTI-MARKET CONFIGURABLE HIGH ENGAGEMENT
📊
Ranking

Users rank selected teams from highest to lowest for a chosen metric — xG, total shots, or any custom stat. Points for each correct position, bonus for a perfect ranking. A unique format that rewards tactical knowledge.

Why sports websites choose Gameweek

Retention
A weekly reason to return

Prediction deadlines create a natural habit loop. Users return before each round to predict, then return again to check results. 38 gameweeks means 38 touchpoints in a single season.

Acquisition

Free-to-play removes the friction that kills sign-up funnels. Users register because the game is fun — not because they've been incentivised. That's a better quality user from day one.

First-party data

Which teams do your users care about? Which competitions do they follow? Prediction patterns reveal fan identity at a level that page views never could. All data stays in your workspace.
